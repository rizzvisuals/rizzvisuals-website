About the photographer

Chasing conditions, not checklists.

Ian Rizzari is a Northern California photographer drawn to coastlines, forests, wildlife, mountains, and the weather that transforms them. His work looks beyond the postcard view toward moments of scale, solitude, tension, and quiet.

Every Signature Work is selected for its ability to hold attention in print—not simply because it records a beautiful place.

“The photograph begins when a familiar place stops looking familiar.”
