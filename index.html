Artist Statement

Chasing the moment a place becomes something more.

Rizzvisuals Photography is centered on atmosphere: marine fog drifting through familiar landmarks, light entering old forests, moonrise over granite, and the brief weather events that transform a landscape.

Rather than documenting places as they usually appear, the work looks for rare conditions that create scale, solitude, and quiet.
